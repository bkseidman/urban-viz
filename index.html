Street Cleaning in San Francisco: Patterns and Frequency

Bodhi Seidman | University of San Francisco

This project explores street sweeping schedules across San Francisco using interactive visualizations. The goal is to make citywide patterns in sweeping frequency, timing, and variation easier to understand.

Project Description

The visualization focuses on three main questions:

  • Where are sweeping patterns concentrated across the city?
  • How often are different streets cleaned?
  • When during the week does sweeping usually occur?

The final site will combine an interactive map, a frequency bar chart, a schedule heatmap, and a street detail panel.
