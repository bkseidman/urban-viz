Street Cleaning in San Francisco: Patterns and Frequency

This beta dashboard explores how street sweeping is distributed across San Francisco. The map is the main view: users can zoom, hover, and click on street segments to inspect individual sweeping schedules. The heatmap and frequency chart provide citywide context.

Interactive Street Sweeping Map

Streets are colored by estimated sweeps per month. Hover for a quick preview, click for full schedule details, and zoom or pan to inspect smaller areas.

Weekday and Time Pattern

Click a heatmap cell to highlight streets swept during that weekday and time bucket.

Frequency Distribution

Click a bar to highlight streets in that estimated sweeps-per-month range.

Most Frequently Swept Streets

This chart shows the street segments with the highest estimated sweeps per month. Click a bar to highlight that street on the map.

Selected Street

Click a colored street segment to view its sweeping schedule.
